ENEMY_SPEED in theme/game_theme.gd: slower makes the game feel calmer and more strategic; faster makes it feel tense and reaction-based.
ENEMY_MAX_COUNT_INITIAL and ENEMY_ESCALATION_INTERVAL in theme/game_theme.gd: more enemies or faster escalation makes pressure build earlier.
ENEMY_HIT_PADDING in theme/game_theme.gd: bigger padding makes collisions feel less forgiving; smaller padding makes dodging feel fairer and more precise.
PLAYER_STARTING_LIVES and LIFE_COLLECTIBLE_CHANCE in theme/game_theme.gd: these change how punishing or generous the game feels.
GRID_ROWS, GRID_COLS, and LETTER_GRID_PADDING in theme/game_theme.gd: more cells gives more movement choices and visual complexity; fewer cells makes choices simpler.
CIRCLE_DOT_COUNT in theme/game_theme.gd: fewer targets creates a shorter round; more targets makes survival matter more.
STAGGER_FRACTION_X and STAGGER_FRACTION_Y in grid/letter_grid.gd: more stagger makes the board feel messy and handmade; less stagger makes it feel cleaner and easier to read.
SCORE_PER_COLLECT and SCORE_PER_SECOND in managers/game_manager.gd: this changes whether the game rewards survival more or collecting more.
